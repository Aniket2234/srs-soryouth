Project Overview

Project Name: Soryouth Solar CRM System

Description: Development of a comprehensive web-based Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system designed specifically for solar installation business operations, managing the complete lifecycle from lead generation through installation, maintenance, and performance monitoring.

Scope of Work Includes:

  • Complete web-based CRM application with responsive design
  • Lead management with multi-channel integration (WhatsApp, Email, SMS)
  • Project tracking and site survey management
  • Document management system with barcode scanning
  • Service ticketing and AMC management
  • Invoicing, billing, and subsidy tracking
  • Vendor and employee management
  • Real-time analytics and reporting dashboard
  • GPS tracking and field operations support
  • Plant monitoring integration
  • Role-based access control and permissions
  • Customer journey and notification system
  • External API integrations (Google Reviews, RMS, Email)
  • Mobile-responsive design for field access
  • Testing, deployment, and documentation
